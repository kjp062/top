Sandwich

Description

A sandwich is two pieces of bread with something in the middle.

Ingredients

  • Bread (2 slices or more)
  • Spread (optional)
  • Topping/Filling (optional, ex. meat or fruit depending on spread)
  1. Lay bread flat
  2. Add spread
  3. Add Topping
  4. put pieces of bread together with spread side facing inwards
